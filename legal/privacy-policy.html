Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 7, 2024

Summary: Save App stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. Your privacy is our priority.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Save App is designed with privacy in mind. All articles, settings, and preferences are stored exclusively on your device using local storage. We have no access to this data.

2. How the App Works

When you save an article:

  • The URL you provide is temporarily processed to extract the article content
  • The extracted content (text, images, metadata) is saved locally on your device
  • No data is stored on our servers
  • No account or login is required

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device:

  • Saved Articles: Stored in your device's local storage
  • App Settings: Stored in your device's local storage
  • Cached Images: Stored in your device's cache directory

You can delete all your data at any time through the app's Settings menu.

4. Third-Party Services

Save App may interact with the following:

  • Websites you save: When you save a URL, we fetch content from that website
  • Text-to-Speech: Uses your device's built-in speech synthesis (no data sent externally)

5. Analytics and Tracking

We do not use any analytics or tracking services.

We do not track your usage, reading habits, or any other behavior within the app.

6. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties.

Since we don't collect data, there is nothing to share.

7. Children's Privacy

Save App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences and contains no age-restricted features.

8. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: View all your saved content within the app
  • Delete: Remove individual articles or all data via Settings
  • Export: Your data remains on your device and under your control

9. Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID). We recommend keeping your device secure to protect your saved content.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.
